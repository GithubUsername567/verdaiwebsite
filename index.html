// Frequently asked

Questions, answered honestly.

Does Verdai really work offline?

Yes. Pro and Co-op tiers include an on-device diagnosis pack covering the 200 most common diseases in your region. Tap once before you head to the field and you're set, even with zero signal.

What counts as a "diagnosis"?

One photo submitted for analysis equals one diagnosis. Follow-up questions to the agronomist about the same scan are unlimited on every paid tier. Re-scans of the same plant within 24 hours don't count twice.

Can I switch tiers or cancel anytime?

Always. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades at the next billing cycle. If you cancel, your data and field journal stay intact — re-subscribe and pick up exactly where you left off.

How is Verdai different from a Google image search?

A search returns thousands of look-alikes; Verdai returns one ranked diagnosis tied to your crop, your stage, and your weather. Then it tells you what to do next, with treatments calibrated for what's actually available where you live.

Do you offer a discount for co-ops in lower-income regions?

Yes — purchasing-power pricing is automatically applied at checkout based on your country, and we offer a sponsored Co-op tier free of charge for verified extension programs. Reach out to partnerships@verdai.com.
